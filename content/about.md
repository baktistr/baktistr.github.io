## Why do I make this blog ?

In my free time, I do a lot of things, such as reading books, doing online courses, as well as doing some labs in the hacking playgrounds such as Hackthebox and Tryhackme. Having Hobbies and then working in the tech industry, I eventually started blogging to verify and confirm my understanding of certain topics, because as the saying goes **_If you can't explain it simply, you don't understand it well enough_**.

As of now, I write this blog to document everything I learn through my road to get certified in OSCP. The main resource of my learning journey is this list of [OSCP-Like machines on Hackthebox from TjNull](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1dwSMIAPIam0PuRBkCiDI88pU3yzrqqHkDtBngUHNCw8/edit#gid=1839402159).

I like to challenge myself and push myself harder toward something. Most of the knowledge I gain is from self-taught and exploration. I indeed believe that writing is one of the most powerful ways to intact the knowledge inside my brain. Furthermore, it's my principle to keep learning and always pursue vast true knowledge.

I also write this blog in order to express my own sincere gratitude to everyone that helps me indirectly with their writing/guide/blog/etc, when I stumble upon some problems. I hope the chain of knowledge doesn't stop here.
