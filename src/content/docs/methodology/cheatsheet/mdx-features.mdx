## Math Equations

Math equations are rendered using KaTeX.

### Inline Math

The probability of success after $n$ attempts is $P = 1 - (1-p)^n$ where $p$ is the probability of success per attempt.

### Block Equations

Shannon entropy for password strength:

$$
H = -\sum_{i=1}^{n} p_i \log_2(p_i)
$$

The time complexity of brute force is:

$$
T(n) = O(k^n)
$$

where $k$ is the keyspace size and $n$ is the key length.

### Cryptography Example

RSA encryption formula:

$$
c = m^e \mod n
$$

RSA decryption:

$$
m = c^d \mod n
$$

where $e$ is the public exponent, $d$ is the private exponent, and $n = p \times q$.

## Combined Example

When calculating the probability of a successful attack chain with $n$ stages:

$$
P_{total} = \prod_{i=1}^{n} P_i
$$

<Mermaid chart={`
graph LR
    A[Stage 1<br/>P=0.8] --> B[Stage 2<br/>P=0.7]
    B --> C[Stage 3<br/>P=0.9]
    C --> D[Success<br/>P=0.504]
`} />

For the above example: $P_{total} = 0.8 \times 0.7 \times 0.9 = 0.504$
